Why HealthierME Challenge ?

The HealthierME challenge is an opportunity for you to prioritize your health and wellbeing, even as you age.

By participating in this challenge, you can avoid spending months in the hospital under doctors’ observation and instead spend quality time with your loved ones.

By participating in this challenge, you will not only improve your health but also contribute to the betterment of the less privileged sections of society.
